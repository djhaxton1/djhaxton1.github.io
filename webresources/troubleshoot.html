INTRODUCTION

If you are unfamiliar with using a drill press, a great way to cover general usage and safety is to watch this great introductory video. It covers use cases where using a drill press is optimal and how to operating it safely and effectively.

To reduce risk of serious injury when using this machine:

  1. Read and understand the manual before starting.
  2. Always wear approved eye protection.
  3. Plug power cord into grounded outlet only.
  4. Tie back long hair, roll up long sleeves, and remove loose clothing, jewelry, or gloves.
  5. Disconnect power before performing maintenance, setup, or service.
  6. DO NOT operate when tired, or under the influence.
  7. Make sure machine is properly adjusted and correctly set up before starting.
  8. Securely bolt machine to floor.
  9. DO NOT use your hands to hold workpiece during operation; clamp workpiece to table.
  10. Use proper speed and cutting tool for the job.
  11. Remove chuck key from chuck before starting
